About this App

Within the minor International Trade Publishing, Books & More (training MIC) at the Amsterdam University of Applied Scinece is a guide made each year for international students coming to study a period in Amsterdam - the International Student Guide Amsterdam (. ISGA) Each student receives the guide. The guide students find practical information and fun tips. The purpose of the guide is that students quickly find their way in and around Amsterdam and a useful and pleasant time experience in the Netherlands. The guide is a physical book.

Because almost everyone has a smartphone these days it is much easier for international students to the ISGA to locate a mobile. This app is also relined to information from the ISGA easy to find. In this app is divided into three parts, namely: Timeline view what Amsterdam students do at this time, Maps easily find the locations of the ISGA around you, and Search the digital lookup of all the ISGA.
